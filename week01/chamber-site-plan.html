Heredia Chamber of Industry, Commerce and Tourism

Site Name

The site name is "Heredia Chamber of Industry, Commerce and Tourism", representing our dedicated focus on promoting business and tourism in Heredia.

Site Purpose

The Heredia Chamber of Industry, Commerce and Tourism supports and promotes local businesses, advocating for favorable business environments, providing networking opportunities, and facilitating resources for growth and development.

Scenarios

  • Benefits for Members: Enhanced business visibility, networking opportunities, advocacy in governmental matters, and access to business and professional development programs.
  • Cost Per Year to Join: Membership fees range from $100 for small businesses to $500 for large enterprises annually.
  • Community Involvement: The Chamber includes a wide range of members from local artisans to large corporations, promoting economic growth and community development in Heredia.

Color Scheme

Headings: Steel Blue #4682B4

Accents: Golden Rod #DAA520

Body Copy: Dark Grey #333333

Typography

The site uses the 'Inter' typeface from Google Fonts, chosen for its clarity and professionalism. The body text is styled with a font weight of 400 (Normal) for readability.
